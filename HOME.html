HISTORY OF H-TOYZ

HI-Toyz was established in 2019 with the vision of creating a haven for toy enthusiasts and collectors in the local community. The founders, passionate collectors themselves, identified the need for a dedicated toy store that catered to the niche market of plastic model kits and figure statues. They believed that toys could be more than just playthings; they could be works of art, expressions of creativity, and sources of joy for collectors of all ages.

From its humble beginnings, HI-Toyz quickly gained recognition and support from the local collector community. With its carefully curated selection of toys from various brands, HI-Toyz became a go-to destination for collectors seeking high-quality and hard-to-find items. The store's commitment to offering a diverse range of products from respected brands such as Tamiya, Bandai, Mattel Toys, Mini-GT, and many others helped establish its reputation as a reliable source for collectors.

Tagline: "Where Collectors Unite, Imagination Takes Flight!"

HI-Toyz's tagline embodies the essence of HI-Toyz as a toy store that goes beyond retail, becoming a place where dreams come to life and lifelong passions are nurtured.
